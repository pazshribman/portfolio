Zigi's Needs

Back to projects

What?

Zigi's needs is a virtual realety expirence, dveloped on oculus quest as part of my graduation project from Bezalel academy, this project was an expeirament of communication between a human beaing to a virtual creature- Zigi.

Why?

Back in 2021, VR was everywere. a lot of tec copmanies puts thir effort on developing this field. in this project, I tried to test the relationships between a human beain to a virtual creature. the player can pet, feed and play with Zigi with a ball. as first steps in the world of design VR experiance and creat relationships inside the virtual world. and expeiramenting on designing clear, interesting and fun experience in this new unknown erea.

Design Challanges

From the designer perspective the main challange was to make Zigi fells a life, with small body movements on idle, and the breating animation from his mouth ans eyes. Zigi is also folowing the player movements, if you will catch a ball he will look at the ball. when the player pet zigi he can feel his shape and vibration, all of this small adjustments togethe makes most of the peapole react to zigi like he is a real creature.

Usabillity

One of The most interesting part in this process was to see how peapole react to the experience from outside every time I observed it become very clear what peapole expect and what I need to change in order to give them the best experince. one of the main example is the movement controller first the player moved with the controller analog but most of the people just use thir legs to move and I went with it, this small change made the experience mounch more realistic. also at first the plan was to play games with Zigi, but most of the people at firt just want to meet him slowly and tring to touch him, so we designed the first expirince to be just get in touch with Zigi and designed diffrent emotions of him on diffrent types of touch, if you will pet him he will be happy and gargling, if user will pounch him he will be mad.

How?

Assets of the project designed with C4D and developed texture and animated inside Unity. powered by oculus quest 2. two talented frends that helps me take this project to the next level
